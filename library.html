College of Engineering and Architecture

Library

The Pangasinan State University- Urdaneta City Campus Library is the central sources of information throughout academic community. It supports the four functions of the University namely Instruction, Research, Extension and Production. Geared to support the academic program of the University, it envisions being a library that facilitates information access to all and plans major role as intermediary of information resources may be in both traditional and electronic format. It’s mission is focused on the provision of quality services, resources and functional information technology- based library services.

Goals

1. To develop and manage the collection that is relevant to the University’s curricular programs and responsive to the needs of students, faculty, researchers, and administrative staff. 2. To organize and preserve information resources that supports the instruction, research and extension, and production of the University. 3. To support and promote the use of information resources by providing references and establishing linkages to meet the needs of the library users. 4. To create and maintain a conducive learning and working environment.

Objectives

1. To be more responsive to the academic needs and research demands of the students, faculty, researchers and administrative staff. 2. To provide a user-friendly and accessible print and non-print collections used in the instruction, research and extension, and production of the University. 3. To conduct and market library programs and services that will promote information literacy. 4. To provide improved physical facilities and up-to-date technical equipment for efficient and effective library services.
